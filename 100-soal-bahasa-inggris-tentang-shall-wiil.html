15. Contoh future tense(wiil)


Contoh Simple Future Tense

(+)Dia (pr) akan di sekolah besok pagi.
She will be in the school tomorrow morning.

(-)Dia (pr) tidak akan di sekolah besok pagi.
She will not be in the school tomorrow morning.

(?)Apakah dia (pr) akan di sekolah besok pagi?
Will She be in the school tomorrow morning?

1. (+) We will go to the cinema tomorrow.
(-) We won't (will not) go to the cinema tomorrow.
(?) Will we go to the cinema tomorrow?

2. (+) You will make a cake for my birthday.
(-) You won't make a cake for my birthday.
(?) Will you make a cake for my birthday?
