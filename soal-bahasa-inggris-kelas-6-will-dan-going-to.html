26. Perbedaan antara I will,I Going to Dan I Would dalam bahasa inggris


i am going to: when something is planned or arranged.
i will: when the future is not planned or you make the decision at the moment of speaking.
i would: you use would to preserve the future aspect when talking about the past.i will;tandanya mengatakan iya,aku akan meakukannya
i going;ya sesuai verbnya,aku pergi
i would;aku akan (pengucapan versi kedua)
