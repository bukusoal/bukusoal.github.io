I have just learnt to

The activities I found most difficult

What I need to do better is/are writing better essay and adding more vocabulary so that I can use my English more naturally.

Penjelasan:

Simple Present Tense

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sehari-hari (Daily routines).  

Kata keterangan yang sering digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

• Every day

• Every week

• every month

• every year

• once a week

• twice a day

• three times a month

• three times a year

• always

• often

• usually

• seldom

• rarely

• dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

(+) S + V1 atau S + V1s/es

(-) S + do/ does + V1

(?) Do/ DOes + S + V1?

NB:

V1 dan Do untuk subject I, You, They, We

V1s/es dan Does untuk subject He, She, It

Jawaban:

1. Dina forgot it was Saturday.

She got up very late, at six. She ran fast to the bathroom. She  thought she would be late to school.  She forgot it was Saturday.  

2. Dina did her routines before school, except  having breakfast.

She took a very quick bath, got dressed, took her bag, and  ran off to school. She did not even have breakfast.  

3. Dina began to realize it was Saturday.  

After walking for five minutes, she noticed that no other  children were going to school.  Soon, she realized it was Saturday!

4. We were all amused.  

So, she walked back home. We all laughed at her when she  got home. She also laughed at herself.

5. Dina got back to bed.

Then she changed her clothes and went back to bed! My naughty sister!

Penjelasan:

Simple Past Tense

Simple Past tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi dan berakhir di masa lampau. Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

1. Yesterday

2. Last night

3. Last week

4. Last month

5. Last year

6. Three days ago

7. Five weeks ago

8. two months ago

9. four years ago

10. this morning

11. this afternoon

12. dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan pada simple past tense adalah:

(+) S + V2

(-) S + did not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1?
