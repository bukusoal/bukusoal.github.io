However, the boy was still breathing. Umar could see that the boy had bruises all over his face and arms. Umar did not know what to do. Few cars passed along this road at this time of the day. 20. contoh soal pts matematika kelas 7 semester 1yang bisa jawab aku kasih point lebih​


1. Cindy mempunyai uang sebesar Rp8.000,00 dan Janet mempunyai uang Rp2.000,00 lebihnya dari uang Cindy. Perbandingan uang Cindy dan Janet adalah ….

a. 2 : 8

b. 8 : 2

c. 4 : 5

d. 5 : 4

Jawaban: c

Pembahasan:

Misalkan uang Cindy = C

              uang Janet = J

Maka diperoleh:

C = 8.000

J = C + 2.000 = 8.000 + 2.000 = 10.000

Sehingga,

C : J = 8.000 : 10.000 = 4 : 5

Jadi, perbandingan uang Cindy dan Janet adalah 4 : 5
