5. Tugasnya membuat PPT Susunannya Slide 1 judul, maman kelompok, fhoto bersama - slide 2 kata pengantar - slide 3 latar belakang -slide 4 perumusan (buat 3:soal) - slide 5&6 materi - slide 7 kesimpulan - slide 8 daftar Pustaka​


Baik, berikut adalah susunan PPT yang diminta:

Slide 1:

- Judul: "Judul Presentasi"

- Maman Kelompok

- Foto bersama kelompok

Slide 2:

- Kata Pengantar:

- Ucapan salam

- Penjelasan tujuan presentasi

- Terima kasih kepada pihak-pihak terkait

Slide 3:

- Latar Belakang:

- Penjelasan tentang latar belakang topik presentasi

- Data atau fakta yang mendukung latar belakang

Slide 4:

- Perumusan:

- Soal 1: [Judul Soal 1]

- Deskripsi soal 1

- Soal 2: [Judul Soal 2]

- Deskripsi soal 2

- Soal 3: [Judul Soal 3]

- Deskripsi soal 3

Slide 5:

- Materi:

- Subjek atau topik 1:

- Penjelasan dan informasi terkait subjek 1

- Subjek atau topik 2:

- Penjelasan dan informasi terkait subjek 2

Slide 6:

- Materi (lanjutan):

- Subjek atau topik 3:

- Penjelasan dan informasi terkait subjek 3

- Subjek atau topik 4:

- Penjelasan dan informasi terkait subjek 4

Slide 7:

- Kesimpulan:

- Ringkasan hasil presentasi

- Poin-poin penting yang disampaikan

- Kesimpulan yang dapat diambil

Slide 8:

- Daftar Pustaka:

- Referensi atau sumber yang digunakan dalam presentasi

- Judul buku, penulis, tahun terbit, dan informasi penerbitan lainnya

Pastikan untuk mengatur tata letak, desain, dan konten setiap slide sesuai dengan preferensi dan kebutuhan presentasi.

25. tolong dibantu jawab semuanya ya untuk slide 1 : judul materi untuk slide 2 : soal materi


1. Correlative Conjunction is a pair of phrases used to connect the sentence elements (phrase/clause) that have the equal information.

2.

~ Either-or is used to present two choices,

~ Both-and is used to emphasize on two true elements in the sentence,

~ Neither-nor is used to negate two choices,

~ Not only-but also is used to emphasize the additional information that seems contradictiory of what we have already known.

3.

➸ Both + NOUN+ and +NOUN

➸ Not only + NOUN + but also + NOUN

➸ Either + NOUN+ or + NOUN

➸ Neither + NOUN+ nor + NOUN

4.

➸ Both + NOUN+ and +NOUN

Both my father and my mother have dinner with me.The game is easy to play for both children and adults.Both English and Math are difficult lessons.

➸ Not only + Adjective + but also + adjective

She is not only beautiful but she's also smart.The car is not only expensive but also cozy.

➸ Either + NOUN+ or + NOUN

Either Nursahel or Winda is asking for the help.Either my father or my sister visits our grandma every week.

➸ Neither + NOUN+ nor + NOUN

Neither Nursahel nor Sinta is in the class now.Neither Nursahel nor his friends enjoy playing the game.

EXPLANATION

Correlative Conjunction is a pair of phrases used to connect the sentence elements (phrase/clause) that have the equal information. It means that we have to use both of them in different places of sentence to make them work. If an adjective follows "either", and then an adjective will also follow "or."

The most commonly used correlative conjunctions are :

➸ Both + ...... + and +....

➸ Not only + .....+ but also + ......

➸ Either + ......+ or + .......

➸ Neither + ....... + nor + ........

The use of correlative conjunction is based on the equal grammatical structure. So, pay attention to the verb agreement, and proper parallel structure.

Exp.

1) Roni doesn't enjoy the beach scenery. Reni doesn't enjoy the beach

Neither Roni nor Reni enjoys the beach scenery.

➸ The sentence above has two subjects, "Roni and Reni." The second subject "Reni" must agree with the verb. Therefore, the suffix -s is added to the verb (enjoys), which adheres to its close singular subject. We must change the negative sentence into the positive form, by omitting the negative auxiliary verb "doesn't."

34. contoh Soal Think Pair Share


Metode TPS (Think Pair Share)

Langkah-langkah Model Pembelajaran Kooperatif

Fase-1 Menyampaikan tujuan dan memotivasi siswa
Tingkah Laku Guru:
Guru menyampaikan semua tujuan pembelajaran yang ingin dicapai pada pelajaran tersebut dan memotivasi siswa untuk belajar.

Fase-2 Menyajikan informasi
Tingkah Laku Guru:

40. apa pengertian dari: slide slide show slide sorter slide master


- Slide adalah satu tampilan di layar berupa uraian, gambar atau grafik yang digunakan untuk mempresentasikan sesuatu

- Slide show adalah jalannya slide yang ditampilkan mulai dari awal slide sampai akhir

- Slide sorter adalah tampilan (view) dalam Ms. Powerpoint yang mengijinkan pemakainya meilhat keseluruhan slide dalam 1 tampilan

- Slide master adalah fitur dalam Powerpoint yang memungkinkan pemakai, membuat template presentasi powerpoint dan mengedit sendiri template powerpoint itu sesuai selera.
