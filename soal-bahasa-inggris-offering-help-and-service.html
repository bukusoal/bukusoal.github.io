4. perbedaan offering service dan help​


Jawaban:

Perbedaan antara offering service dan help adalah sebagai berikut:

1. Offering service (menawarkan layanan):

Offering service merujuk pada tindakan atau upaya untuk menawarkan atau menyediakan layanan kepada orang lain. Hal ini umumnya dilakukan oleh perusahaan atau individu yang memiliki keahlian atau keterampilan khusus dalam suatu bidang tertentu. Pada umumnya, tawaran layanan ini berupa penawaran untuk melakukan pekerjaan atau menghasilkan sesuatu bagi orang lain dengan imbalan tertentu.

Contoh offering service: Seorang tukang ledeng menawarkan jasanya untuk memperbaiki keran yang rusak atau seorang fotografer profesional menawarkan layanan fotografi pernikahan.

2. Help (membantu):

Help atau membantu merujuk pada tindakan atau upaya untuk memberikan bantuan atau sokongan kepada orang lain yang membutuhkan. Tujuan utama dari membantu adalah untuk memberikan dukungan atau solusi kepada orang yang membutuhkan, tanpa adanya imbalan atau persyaratan tertentu.

Contoh help: Memberi tumpangan kepada seorang teman yang membutuhkan, membantu orang asing menemukan jalan, atau memberikan saran kepada seseorang yang mengalami masalah pribadi.

Dalam ringkasan, offering service melibatkan penawaran atau penyediaan layanan berdasarkan keahlian atau keterampilan tertentu, sementara help melibatkan memberikan bantuan kepada orang lain tanpa imbalan khusus yang dituntut.

11. buatlah rankuman Asking and Offering Help / service – Definition, Usage, Examples, Dialogues kedalam bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Asking and Offering Help / Service – Definition, Usage, Examples, Dialogues

Definition:

Asking and offering help or service refers to the act of seeking assistance or providing assistance to others in various situations. It involves expressing a willingness to support or lend a hand to someone in need.

Usage:

Asking and offering help or service is commonly used in both formal and informal settings. It can be utilized in personal conversations, professional interactions, customer service scenarios, or when volunteering to assist others.

Examples:

1. Asking for Help:

- Could you please help me carry these boxes to the car?

- I'm having trouble understanding this math problem. Can you explain it to me?

- Excuse me, could you show me the way to the nearest post office?

- I'm feeling unwell. Can you get me a glass of water, please?

2. Offering Help:

- If you need any assistance with your project, feel free to ask.

- I noticed you're struggling with that task. Would you like me to lend a hand?

- Let me know if there's anything I can do to help make your event a success.

- If you ever need someone to talk to, I'm here for you.

Dialogues:

1. Asking for Help:

A: Hey, I'm having some trouble setting up my new laptop. Could you help me with it?

B: Of course! I have experience with that. What seems to be the problem?

2. Offering Help:

A: I heard you're moving to a new apartment next week. Do you need any help with packing?

B: That would be fantastic! I have a lot of stuff to sort through. Your assistance would be greatly appreciated.

3. Asking for Help:

A: I'm running late for a meeting and can't find my car keys. Can you help me search for them?

B: Absolutely! Let's check the usual spots first and see if we can locate them quickly.

4. Offering Help:

A: I noticed you've been working on that report for hours. Do you want me to review it and provide feedback?

B: That would be fantastic! I could use a fresh pair of eyes to ensure it's error-free. Thank you!

Remember, asking for help when needed and offering assistance to others creates a supportive and collaborative environment in both personal and professional relationships.

36. 1. Write the patterns of offering help /service 2. Write possible responses for offering help / service


Offering help/service is one of the most common expressions used in English. We can use this when we want to offer our help to other people.

Pembahasan

Patterns of offering help/service are

1. May + I + verb 1?

For example:

May I help you?May I give you a hand?

2. Would you like me to + verb 1?

For example:

Would you like me to cook our dinner?Would you like me to bring your books to the class?

3. Can + I + verb 1?

For example:

Can I help you?Can I bring this to your table?

4. Do you want me + to + verb 1?

For example:

Do you want me to write this letter for you?Do you want me to iron your clothes?

Possible responses for offering help/service.

Thank you. That would be really helpful.Yes, that would be great!Thank you. I would appreciate it.

Responses for rejecting offer:

It's okay, thank you. I can manage it myself.Thanks but don't worry, I can do it.I appreciate it but don't worry, I'm about to do it. Pelajari lebih lanjut

Jawaban:

Mapel: B.InggrisKelas: 7 SMP

Pertanyan:

Soal Essay:buat dialog offering help and service di minimarket untuk dua orangJawaban Dari Soal Essay Diatas Adalah:Seller: Good morning, do you need help?

Buyer: Yes, I need to buy a bread for breakfast. Can you give me a recommend?

Seller: Sure! Cheese bread with blueberry sauce is popular here. do you want to try?

Buyer: Okay, I will buy it

Seller: Do you need a paper bag?

Buyer: No, thank you

Terjemahan:Penjual: Selamat pagi, apakah kamu butuh bantuan?

Pembeli: Ya, saya harus membeli roti untuk sarapan. Bisakah kamu memberi rekomendasi?

Penjual: Tentu! Roti keju dengan saus blueberry populer di sini. Apakah kamu ingin mencobanya?

Pembeli: Oke, saya akan membelinya

Penjual: Apakah kamu membutuhkan tas kemasan?
