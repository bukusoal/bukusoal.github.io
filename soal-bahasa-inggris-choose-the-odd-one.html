11. Choose the expression of leave taking. Tolong dijawab ya kak yang pinter bahasa inggris


The correct expression of leave taking for that question is :

C. Sorry, I should go now - See YouPEMBAHASAN

Leave Taking

Salam yang diucapkan saat hendak melakukan perpisahan. Kata-kata dapat dirangkai berdasarkan variasi waktu perpisahan dengan orang tersebut. Kata-kata ini diucapkan sebagai salam dan doa.

DITANYAKAN

Choose the expression of leave takings.

A. Good morning - Good morningB. Nice to meet you - Nice to meet you too.C. Sorry, I should go now - See you.D. Don't go - SurePENJELASAN

A. Good morning - Good morning

Opsi A adalah Incorrect.Terjemahan : Selamat pagi - Selamat pagi.Salam ini digunakan untuk menyapa di awal perjumpaan.

B. Nice to meet you - Nice to meet you too.

Opsi B adalah Incorrect.Terjemahan : Senang berkenalan dengan anda - Senang berkenalan dengan anda juga.Salam ini diucapkan saat berkenalan dengan orang baru.

C. Sorry, I should go now - See you.

Opsi C adalah Correct.Terjemahan : Maaf, saya harus pergi sekarang - Sampai Jumpa.Salam ini diucapkan saat akan berpisah atau di akhir perjumpaan.

D. Don't go - Sure

Opsi D adalah Incorrect.Terjemahan : Jangan pergi - Baiklah.Salam ini tidak diucapkan untuk berpisah, melainkan mencegah perpisahan untuk terjadi.PELAJARI LEBIH LANJUTFarewell Greetings https://brainly.co.id/tugas/7305712Daily Greetings https://brainly.co.id/tugas/10261575Good Night Messages https://brainly.co.id/tugas/17908813

8. A. Should be careful

10. C. will be

11. C. will

Jawaban:

1. E. Should not have eaten - The correct answer is "should not have eaten" because it refers to something that Abi regrets doing in the past, which is eating spicy food at Dira's party.

2. D. should be cooking - The correct answer is "should be cooking" because it indicates an ongoing action in the present. Lala intended to cook the food but couldn't due to having a fever.

3. A. Should - The correct answer is "Should" because it implies a recommendation or advice to be careful in handling the expensive Chinese vase. The sentence doesn't indicate any negation.

4. C. should not give - The correct answer is "should not give" because it advises against giving the baby a pen to play with due to the potential risks involved.

5. D. should not smoke - The correct answer is "should not smoke" because it advises against smoking for someone who is coughing a lot.

6. A. Should consult - The correct answer is "Should consult" because it suggests consulting a dictionary as a recommended action for understanding the meaning.

7. B. He shouldn't be driving over the limit - The correct answer is "He shouldn't be driving over the limit" because it conveys the idea that Tomy should not exceed the speed limit while driving.

8. A. Should be careful - The correct answer is "Should be careful" because it advises being cautious when crossing the street.

9. C. should have - The correct answer is "should have" because it implies that Dika should have studied for the biology test by now.

10. A. Will meet - The correct answer is "Will meet" because it indicates a future action of meeting us at the bus stop.

11. C. will - The correct answer is "will" because it asks about a future action of going somewhere.

12. B. will do - The correct answer is "will do" because Lara expresses her belief that she will perform well on the test in the future.

Jawaban:

Percakapan 1

Emily : Hi Tom! What are you doing?

Tommy : I’m reading a newspaper.

Emily : Any good news?

Tommy : I’ve just read about full day school.

Emily : So what your opinion about that?

Tommy : In my view, it is useful for Indonesian education development. Because if school time is extended until 4 or 5 PM, students can learn more. So, Indonesia’s educational ranking in the world can be increased.

Emily : Do you think like that? But how about the students? Don’t you think it is too boring for them? In school all day.

Tommy : No, If the teacher can bring class situation more fun, giving them exciting activities, so the student will enjoy their day in school.

Emily : I think you’re right

Terjemahan:

Emily : Hai Tom! Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan?

Tommy : Saya sedang membaca koran.

Emily : Ada kabar baik?

Tommy : Saya baru saja membaca tentang sekolah sehari penuh.

Emily : Jadi bagaimana pendapat Anda tentang itu?

Tommy : Dalam pandangan saya, ini berguna untuk perkembangan pendidikan Indonesia. Karena jika waktu sekolah diperpanjang hingga jam 4 atau 5 sore, siswa dapat belajar lebih banyak. Jadi, peringkat pendidikan Indonesia di dunia dapat ditingkatkan.

Emily : Apakah Anda berpikir seperti itu? Tapi bagaimana dengan siswa? Tidakkah menurut Anda itu terlalu membosankan bagi mereka? Di sekolah sepanjang hari.

Tommy : Tidak, jika guru dapat membuat situasi kelas lebih menyenangkan, memberi mereka kegiatan yang menyenangkan, sehingga siswa akan menikmati hari mereka di sekolah.

Emily : Saya pikir Anda benar.
