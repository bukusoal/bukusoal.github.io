PEMANGKATAN ADALAH SUATU BENTUK OPERASI MATEMATIKA YG PENGERJAAN NYA BERULANG-ULANG ATAU DI SEBUT PERKALIAN GANDA

SOAL ↓

1) 4! + 6!

→ [ 4.3.2.1 ] + [ 6.5.4.3.2.1 ]

→ 24 + 720

→ 744

2) 3² + 3⁵

→ [ 3.3 ] + [ 3.3.3.3.3 ]

→ 9 + 243

→ 252

1. 4! + 6!

= (4×3×2×1) + (6×5×4×3×2×1)

= (12×2) + (30×4×3×2×1)

= 24 + (120×3×2×1)

= 24 + (360×2)

= 24 + 720

= 740 ☑

2. 3² + 3⁵

= (3×3) + ({3×3}×{3×3}×3)

= 9 + ({9×9}×3)

= 9 + (81×3)

= 9 + 243

= 252 ☑
