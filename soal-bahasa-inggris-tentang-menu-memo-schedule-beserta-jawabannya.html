9. tolong bantu bikin dialog tentang memo,menu, schedule,sign tempat nya di cafe 3 orang,bantu ya gess​


Jawaban:

**Dialog about Memo, Menu, Schedule, and Sign in a Café for 3 People**

*Characters:*

- **Café Manager (CM)**

- **Chef (Ch)**

- **Waitstaff (W)**

---

**Scene: Café Manager's Office**

**CM:** Good morning, team. We've got a few things to cover today. Firstly, I've drafted a memo regarding the upcoming promotional events. (Shows the memo)

**Ch:** (Nods) Sure, what's the plan?

**CM:** We're introducing a new coffee blend next week, so the memo outlines the details. Make sure all staff members are informed.

**W:** (Hands over a schedule) Here's the schedule for the upcoming week, including shifts for the new promotion.

**CM:** (Takes the schedule) Thanks. Ensure everyone gets a copy. Chef, we've received some feedback about diversifying our menu.

**Ch:** (Enthusiastic) Great! What are we thinking?

**CM:** (Points to the menu) I'd like to add a couple of vegan options and perhaps a signature dessert. Can you come up with some ideas for those?

**Ch:** Absolutely, I'll start experimenting with recipes right away.

**W:** (Points to a sign) Manager, the outdoor sign near the entrance needs to be updated. It's looking a bit worn out.

**CM:** (Takes note on the memo) Good catch. We'll get that sorted this week. Anything else to discuss?

**Ch:** (Shakes head) I'm good.

**W:** (Nods) Same here.

**CM:** Alright then, let's keep up the great work. If there are any more ideas or concerns, feel free to bring them up. Meeting adjourned.

12. tolong bantu buat dialog tentang memo,menu, schedule and sign dongg 3 orang bsk di kumpulin​


Jawaban:

**Dialog about Memo, Menu, Schedule, and Sign for 3 People**

*Characters:*

- **Diana** (Restaurant Manager)

- **Adi** (Chef)

- **Rika** (Waitress)

---

**Scene: In the Restaurant Manager's Office**

**Diana:** (Reviewing a memo) Good morning, everyone. There are a few things we need to discuss. First, I've prepared a memo about changes to the restaurant's operating hours. (Shows the memo)

**Adi:** (Nods) Okay, Mba. What are the changes?

**Diana:** (Points to the memo) Our opening hours will be moved up by one hour starting next week. Make sure all employees are aware of this.

**Rika:** (Hands over a schedule) Mba Diana, here's the waitstaff schedule for next week. It's adjusted according to the new operating hours.

**Diana:** (Takes the schedule) Great, Rika. Ensure all waitstaff understand this change. Next, Adi, we need to add some new dishes to our menu.

**Adi:** (Excited) What are they, Mba Diana?

**Diana:** (Points to the menu) I want to add a new pasta dish and a seafood dish. Can you create the recipes and display them on the menu?

**Adi:** Certainly, Mba. I'll get the ingredients sorted right away.

**Rika:** (Points to the sign) Mba Diana, our menu board needs a refresh. Some parts have faded.

**Diana:** (Jots down in the memo) Duly noted. The rest, let's discuss this week's specials and promotional items for social media. Meeting adjourned.


13. memo bahasa inggris tentang legenda beserta aritnya


In a hill far from the village, in Kalimantan area there lived a poor widow and a daughter. The widow's daughter is very beautiful. But unfortunately, he has a very bad behavior. The girl was very lazy, never helped her mother do the chores. It only works every day. Besides the slacker, the girl was spoiled. All his requests must be obeyed. Every time he asked for something to his mother must be granted, regardless of the situation of his poor mother, every day have to drudge to find a bite of rice. One day the girl's daughter took her mother down to the village to shop. The location of the village market is very far away, so they have to walk quite tiring. The girl walks around in nice clothes and preening so that the path people who see her will admire her beauty. While her mother walked behind her carrying a basket with a very dirty clothes. Because they live in a remote place, nobody knows that the two women who are walking are mothers and children.
As they began to enter the village, the villagers looked at them. They were so fascinated by the girl's beauty, especially the unsatisfied young villagers looking at her face. But when he saw the man walking behind the girl, it really contrasts. It makes people wonder. Among the people who saw it, a young man approached and asked the girl, "Hi, pretty girl. Is that behind your mother? " However, what is the girl's answer? "No," he said arrogantly. "He is my assistant!" The two mothers and children then went on their way. Not that far, approach a young man again and ask the girl. "Hi sweety. Is it your mother who walked behind you? " "No, no," the girl replied, tilting her head back. "He is a slave!" That's how every girl meets someone along the street asking about her mother, always the answer. Her mother was treated as her maid or slave. At first hearing her daughter's rebellious reply when asked by the person, the mother can still restrain herself. But after repeatedly hearing the same and very painful answer, finally the poor mother could not help herself. The mother prayed. "My God, I can not resist this insult. The servant's son is so self-righteous to treat himself as such. Yes, god punish this ungodly child! Punish him ... "
On the power of God Almighty, slowly the body of the ungodly girl turned to stone. change starts from feet. When the change had reached half the body, the girl cried for forgiveness to her mother. "Oh, Mother..mim .. forgive me, forgive your child's iniquity for so long. Mother ... Mother ... forgive your son .. "The girl kept wailing and crying begging her mother. But it was too late. The whole body of the girl eventually turned to stone. Despite being a stone, one can see that both eyes still shed tears, as if in tears. Therefore, the stone that comes from the girl who gets his mother's curse is called "Crying Stone".

36. pengertian dari memo schedule menu dan sign or simbol ​


• Memo adalah pesan yang ditulis secara singkat, jelas dan mudah dimengerti.

• Schedule adalah daftar kegiatan, bisa berupa jadwal acara, jadwal pelajaran, dll.

• Menu adalah daftar makanan atau minuman, bisa juga diartikan perintah perangkat lunak

• Sign or simbol adalah tanda petunjuk, instruksi atau larangan
