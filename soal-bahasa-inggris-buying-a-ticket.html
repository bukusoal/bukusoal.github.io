12. tolong bantuMake 8 paragraph about going to cinema and buying ticket movie​


Jawaban:

One of the most popular types of entertainment is film. The audience will pause for a minute or two and nibble on shoddy nourishment or in simple words, junk food while the film plays and takes us on a thrilling ride through someone else's life story.

Entertainment shifted from being something you paid to see in a theatre or other public venue to something you could appreciate from the comfort of your own home thanks to technological advancements. Because of these technological advancements, it is argued that more people are inclined towards watching movies on television rather than going to the movies. Thus, the theatre industry will be severely affected. I agree with the viewpoint that the movie industry would suffer as a result of the introduction of new means to watch movies. This doesn't mean I go to the cinema rarely. Quite the opposite, actually.

We all have our favorite movies. In my opinion, favorites usually entail an incident or a tale that is difficult to forget and makes an impression on you. My favorite movie is no different. The first time I watched it (my favorite movie) was also the first time I went to the cinema.

I still remember it vividly. I was nine years old.

On the day of my birthday, my mother surprised me. She took me and my big brother to the cinema.

Stepping my little foot into the cinema, I could smell the popcorns and other foods that were being made and served. We walked to the ticket seller (or cashier) and purchased three tickets to the movie "Paulie". With a smile on her face, my mother bought me and my big brother popcorns. It truly was a memorable day, digging my fingers through a box of hot, buttered, salted popcorn in a dark movie theatre with my beloved ones on my special day for the very first time!

The movie "Paulie" was terrific, to say the least! "Paulie" is a sweet, sensitively handled tale about the bond between a bird and its owner, with some great lessons about friendship. It's about a clever parrot caged in a research lab who tells his life story to a lonely janitor. Before coming to the science lab, he has passed through the hands of a succession of owners, from a petty criminal to a man who runs a parrot-dancing act. Touched by his eccentric story, the janitor conspires to liberate the talking bird.

Now I'm an adult and I love going to the cinema. I usually go to the cinema once a month. I will always remember the day that I went to the cinema for the first time.

Penjelasan:

Note that "Paulie" is just an example, you can change it to other movies if you'd like. So you can describe the rest paragraphs yourself.
