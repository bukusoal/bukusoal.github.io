3. contoh teks prosedur makanan atau minuman dalam bahasa inggris


How To Make Chicken Soup (Cara Membuat Sop Ayam)

Chicken soup is the soup that favorites by most of people, besides the delicious taste it also has many benefit for our body. If we want to learn about how to make chicken soup, we need to follow these following steps of recipe:

Ingredients:

1. 350 grams chicken chunk

2. 2carrots

3. 2 potatoes

4. 5 stalks celery

5. 1 red tomato

6. 1 cup of water

7. 2 spoons of cooking oil for sauteing

Spices:

1. 5 onions

2. 2 garlic

3. 3 candlenuts

4. ½ teaspoon of pepper

5. ½ tablespoon of salt

6. ½ teaspoon of sugar

Procedures

1. Combine the chicken and water. Clean the chicken then drain it.

2. Turn on the stove, stir-fry the spices and cooking oil.

3. Add water and wait until it boiling, then add the chicken.

4. While chicken is simmering, prepare the vegetables: peel the carrots and potatoes. Chop into a few chunks.

5. Add the vegetables to the soup.Roughly 30 minutes then add tomato and celery.

6. Turn off the stove and chicken soup is ready to be served.

How To Make Apple Juice (Cara Membuat Jus Apel)

Apple is a fruit that rich of vitamin A which is very useful for the healthy of eyes. So, it is important for us to consume apple regularly. Besides can be eaten directly, apples can also be consumed by making it into juice. Here are the steps on how to make apple juice.

Ingredients:

1.an apple

2.½ glass of water

3.½ glass of ice pack

4.2 tablespoons of sugar

Procedures

1. Peel the apple and clean it.

2. Cut the apple into pieces and put them into juicer.

3. Add water, ice and sugar.

4. Turn on the juicer. Roughly 30 seconds then turn off.

5. Pour the juice into glass and apple juice is ready to drink.

Ingredients:

1 frozen banana1 cup greens (baby spinach, kale, collard, chard, etc.)1 cup unsweetened milk (nut,soy, dairy)Honey or maple syrup, to taste

Steps:

Wash all the greens firstly until everything is cleanAdd one by one the ingredients on to the blenderBlend it with high speed until the texture become smooth.Serve while its cold.
