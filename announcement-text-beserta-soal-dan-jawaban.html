1. contoh text announcement beserta definisinya


Pengertian Teks Announcement
Setiap belajar jenis teks bahasa Inggris, selalu kita awali dengan pengertian atau definisi jenis teks tersebut. Hal ini penting karena tiap jenis teks mempunyai tujuan dan struktur berbeda. Dengan mengetahui pengertiannya, akan sangat terbantu dalam memahami keseluruhan teks tersebut meski disana sini ada banyak kosa kata (vocabulary) yang kita belum tahu artinya.
Menurut para ahli, seperti meriam-webster.com, kata announcement didefinisikan sebagi berikut:

a written or spoken statement that tells people about something : public or formal words that announce something

Kalau kita merujuk pada definisi tersebut, bisa kita sederhanakan bahwa announcement atau pengumuman adalah pernyataan atau tulisan resmi yang memberitahukan kepada khalayak umum tentang sesuatu.
Karena fungsi utamanyanya adalah memberitahukan, menyerukan dan mengajak, maka annoucement ini sangat berkaitan dengan jenis teks analytical exposition dan juga hortatory text.

Thank you,

Mr. Will Pada 13 Desember kita akan berkemah ke situs kemah di Tengah Inggris untuk alasan edukasi.

Terima kasih,

Pak Will

37. Kalimat pertanyaan dan 5 jawabannya dari materi announcement text!​


1. What is announcement text?

2. What is the purpose of announcement text?

3. What is the structure of announcement text?

4. What is the language features of announcement text?

5. Write one announcement text!

Jawabannya

1. Announcement is a statement that contains information about events or events that will occur for the general public to know.

2. Announcements are made with the aim of announcing important information that must be known by many people.

3. The announcement text consists of three parts, namely Opening, Content, and Closing.

Opening is the opening part of the announcement text. The opening section contains title as well as the purpose of making the announcement or outline of information which will be conveyed in the announcement.Content is part of the contents of the announcement text. The content section contains complete information about what is to be conveyed to the general public through the announcement. For example, if you want to announce an event, it means that the content section will contain the time and place for the event, as well as other provisions regarding the event, for example the dress code that must be worn.Closing is the closing part of the announcement text. The closing section contains information about a contact person who can be contacted regarding the announcement if the reader needs further information. In this section, you can also include the website or social media of the announced event. In the announcement example above, the closing section contains information on the website page for registering for the competition, as well as a contact person to contact regarding the competition.

4. The language features of announcement text:

The use of imperative sentences.The use of simple present tense.The use of simple future tense.
