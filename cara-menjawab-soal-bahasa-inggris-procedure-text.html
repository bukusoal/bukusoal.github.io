4. pengertian procedure text dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its social function is to describehow something is completely done through a sequence of series. Communicative purpose of this text is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps.


Teks prosedur adalah teks yang menjelaskan atau membantu kita bagaimana membuat atau menggunakan sesuatu. Fungsi sosialnya adalah menggambarkan sesuatu yang benar-benar dilakukan melalui serangkaian urutan. Tujuan komunikatif teks ini adalah untuk menggambarkan bagaimana sesuatu dibuat melalui serangkaian tindakan atau langkah.

KELAS : 9

KODE KATEGORISASI : 9.5.4


PEMBAHASAN

================

Untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas jawaban yang tepat adalah...

(A) will get

Alasannya adalah karena kalimat di atas bentuk CS Type 1 dimana setelah "if you do" disini kata kerjnya menggunakan V1, So conditional nya harus will + V1 "will get"


Karena rumus conditional type 1

If + simple present, conditional (will+V1)

Kalimat ini adalah contoh kalimat First Conditional/Conditional Sentence Type 1, diketahui dari tense yang digunakan setelah kata "if", yaitu Simple Present Tense (you do this test well).

Maka klausa setelahnya(setelah tanda koma), menggunakan Simple Future Tense. (you will get a good mark).

Rumus First Conditional: If + (simple present tense), (simple future tense).

JAWABANNYA: A

30. persamaan dan perbedaan procedure text dan manual procedure (text dalam bahasa Inggris) ​


Jawaban:

helloooo saya bantu jawab yah :)

PERSAMAAN (equality/sameness)

1. General Purpose (tujuan umum): Both procedure text and manual procedure have the general purpose of providing instructions or guidance on how to perform a specific action or process.

2. Similar Structure (struktur serupa): They both have a similar structure, consisting of steps or a sequence of actions that must be followed by the reader or user.

DIFFERENCE (perbedaan)

1. Content and Expertise (konten dan keahlian): Procedure Text is for everyday tasks like cooking or personal care and is easy to understand. Manual Procedure is for technical or professional tasks, often requiring specialized knowledge.

2. Context (konteks): Procedure Text is used in everyday situations, while Manual Procedure is used in technical or industrial settings where precision and safety are critical.

1. Prepare land for planting, then dig a hole that is deep enough up to 80 centimeters. Clean the planting area so that there are no remnants of bricks or anything that can inhibit the growth of this plant.

2. Choose a frangipani parent with a large size, old age and healthy or without a disability. Then cut the frangipani stems around 20 cm. After that, leave it for one week and then place it in a damp shade.

3. For the upper stem if you have a lot of leaves, remove the leaves and leave only 2 leaves so that evaporation is reduced when the tuning is done.

4. After the trunk is cut, dip or rub the scars from the cut with root stimulants in the farm shop as soon as possible to stimulate rapid root growth.

5. For planting media, use fuel husks mixed with charcoal and stir until blended.

37. cari tentang procedure text lengkap dalam bahasa inggris


The purpose of a procedure is to tell the reader how to do or

make something. The information is presented in a logical

sequence of events, which is broken up into small sequenced

steps. The most common example of a procedural text is a

recipe.

Types of Procedural Texts

Texts that instruct how to do a particular activity ; recipes, rules

for games, science experiments, road safety rules, how to do it

manuals. Texts that instruct how to operate things how to operate

an appliance, a machine, the photocopier, the computer

Features A procedure usually has four components

Goal or Aim -states what is to be done

Materials, Ingredients or Equipment- listed in order of use, includes items needed

to complete task

Steps—a series of steps

Evaluation or Conclusion- how the success of the procedure can be tested

**Procedure: How to Make a Cup of Tea**

*Introduction:*
Making a cup of tea is a simple and comforting process. Follow these steps to brew a perfect cup of tea.

**Materials Needed:**
1. A tea bag or loose tea leaves
2. A cup or mug
3. Boiling water
4. A teaspoon
5. A saucer (optional)
6. Sugar, honey, or milk (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Boil Water:
- Start by boiling water in a kettle. Make sure it's fresh and cold.

2. Preheat the Cup:
- While the water is boiling, pour a small amount of hot water into your cup to preheat it. Swirl it around and then discard the water.

3. Add the Tea Bag or Tea Leaves:
- If using a tea bag, place it into the cup. If using loose tea leaves, add one teaspoon of tea leaves to the cup.

4. Pour Hot Water:
- Once the water reaches a rolling boil, pour it into the cup over the tea bag or tea leaves.

5. Steep the Tea:
- Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes. The longer you steep, the stronger the flavor.

6. Remove the Tea Bag or Strain the Tea:
- If you used a tea bag, remove it from the cup. If you used loose tea leaves, strain the tea to remove them.

7. Customize Your Tea (Optional):
- Add sugar, honey, or milk to your tea according to your preference. Stir well.

8. Serve:
- Place a saucer under the cup (if you like), and serve your tea hot.

9. Enjoy:
- Find a cozy spot, sip your tea, and savor the soothing flavor.

1. What is the first step in making tea?
