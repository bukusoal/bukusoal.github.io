11. buatkan cerita yang mencakup simple present, present continous, present perfect, present perfect continous, simple past, past continous, past perfect, past perfect continous


Explanation:

- Simple present tense
This tense is used to tell about an action that happens at the present time or habitual activity. It also can be used to tell about general truth.
The structure: S + V1 (+ s/es) + O

- Present continuous tense
This tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + O

- Present perfect tense
This tense is used to tell that the action is completely done in an unspecific time before now.
The structure: S + has/have + V3 + O

- Present perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to tell that the action starts at an unspecific time in the past and continue to the present.
The structure: S + has/have + been + O

- Simple past tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that is happened in the past. It began and ended in the past.
The structure: S + V2 + O

- Past continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that was in progress at a particular time in the past.
The structure: S + to be (was/were) + V-ing + O

- Past perfect tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was completely done before another time in the past.
The structure: S + had + V3 + O

- Past perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was in progress before another event in the past. This tense is used to show the duration of the first event.
The structure: S + had + been + V-ing + O

Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. When I faced behind I saw a shadow and went into a coma for two whole days. When I woke up I realized that shadow was my own shadow.
