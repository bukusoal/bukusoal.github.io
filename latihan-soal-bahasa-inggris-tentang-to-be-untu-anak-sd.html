Beno      : “ Really?” I don’t believe it.”
Ana        : “ Yes ofcourse, Beno. I didn’t lie to you.”
Beno      : “ Where you know about the score of english in national exams?”
Ana        : “ Hmm... From someone “.
Beno      : “ Who is? He/she is a teacher?”.
Ana        : “ Oh no. Guessing, please?”
Beno     : “ It’s my friends?”
Ana        : “ Yes ofcourse”.
Beno     : “ Who is ? girl or boy?”
Ana        : “ It’s girl. She is beautiful and smart girl in my class.”
Beno     : “ Ohh, she is Lala”.
Ana        : “ It’s true. I’m proud of you. You're so smart”.
Beno     : “ Thanks Ana “. Please, don’t be over praissing me.”
Ana        : “ Come with me to class, please?”
Beno     : “ It’s okey”.
Ana        : “ Surprise “.
Beno     : “ Oh My God, this is the happiest surprise of my life. Thank you Ana, you’re my best friend.”
Ana        : “ You’rewelcome, Beno.”

40. Mention some expressions of thanking! (sebutkan contoh ungkapan-ungkapan untuMenyatakan terima kasih dalam bahasa inggris ) :​


Jawaban:

Expression of thanking:

1. Thanks (Makasih)

2. Thank you very much (Terima kasih banyak)

3. Thank’s a lot (Terima kasih banyak)

4. I owe you one (Aku berhutang sekali padamu/Terima kasih)

5. Thank’s a bunch (Terima kasih banyak)

Atau dalam bentuk formal kita juga dapat menggunakan frasa berikut:

1. I really appreciate (Aku sangat menghargainya)

2. I will never forget what you’ve done (Aku tidak akan pernah melupakannya)

3. That’s very kind of you (Kamu baik sekali)

4. I’m (extremely) grateful for… (Aku berterima kasih/bersyukur atas…)

5.Thank you in advance (Terima kasih sebelumnya)

6. Thank you beforehand (Terima kasih sebelumnya)

7. Thank’s before (Terima kasih sebelumnya)
