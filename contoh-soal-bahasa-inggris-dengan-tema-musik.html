18. Quiz.Bahasa inggris Apa ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Dimana ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Kapan ?Contoh kalimat ?bahasa inggris Siapa ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Mengapa ?Contoh Kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Bagaimana ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris berapa ?Contoh kalimat ?Nt : First ? akhirnya dapat bagian pertama​


Jawaban:

1. Bahasa inggris Apa ? What

Contoh kalimat ? “What made you happy today?”

2. Bahasa inggris Dimana ? Where

Contoh kalimat ? “Where is a suitable place for a picnic?”

3. Bahasa inggris Kapan ? When

Contoh kalimat ? “When did you come back from Surabaya?”

4. Bahasa inggris Siapa ? Who

Contoh kalimat ? "Who picked the flower?"

5. Bahasa inggris Mengapa ? Why

Contoh Kalimat ? “Why are you crying?"

6. Bahasa inggris Bagaimana ? How

Contoh kalimat ? “How are you today?”

7. Bahasa inggris berapa ? How many / How much

Contoh kalimat ? “How much does that book cost?”

It is about learning how to think critically, solve problems, and communicate effectively. It is about developing the creativity and innovation that is needed to drive progress and prosperity.

But education is not just about the individual, it is also about the collective. Education is the foundation of our democracy and the key to building a just and equitable society. It is the means by which we can ensure that everyone has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Unfortunately, not all students have the same opportunities to access quality education. But it is important to remember that education is the key to unlocking the potential of each and every student. Therefore, we should work together to ensure that every student has access to the resources and support they need to succeed.

In conclusion, education is one of the most powerful tools that we have to shape our future. It is crucial for the development of our society and for building a just and equitable society. Let's work together to ensure that every student has access to quality education and the opportunity to succeed.
