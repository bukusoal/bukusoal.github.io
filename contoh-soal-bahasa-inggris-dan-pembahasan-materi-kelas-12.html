5. contoh penjelasan materi bahasa inggris tentang weather, tolong bantu dong


wheater??
in this world has many weather such as autumn, spring, snow, and summer. whereas in Indonesian has 2 kind weather like rain and summer.
that's all of my explanation hopes u can know what I mean. Rumus kalimat ketika membicarakan tentang weather = It is + Adjective

Contoh:
It is *sunny* today (Hari yang *cerah*)
It is hot today (Hari yang *panas*)

Berikut contoh musim dalam bahasa inggris:
Rainy = Hujan
Sunny = Cerah
Windy = Berangin
Cloudy = Berawan
Foggy = Berkabut
Snowy = Bersalju
Hot = Panas
Cold = Dingin

Contoh kalimat menggunakan kata kata musim:
- It's *rainy* right now, you have to take an umbrella (Sekarang *hujan*, kau harus mengambil payung)
- It's usually *hot* in Jakarta, But now it's *sunny* (Biasanya panas di jakarta, tapi sekarang cerah)

Contoh pertanyaan tentang musim:
- What's the weather like in Bandung in January (Bagaimana cuaca di bandung di bulan januari?)

30. materi caption bahasa inggris wajib kelas 12


Kelas : XII
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Caption
Kata kunci : social function, structure and language features, message, 

Pembahasan:
Karena soal kurang lengkap, saya akan menjawab soal reflection halaman 25 dari buku kela XII.

1) What is the social function of captions?
The social function of caption is helping the reader or viewer to understand information that may not be in a photo or a video.

2) What the structure and language features used in captions?
The structure used in captions:
- the little
- the lead
- section headings
The language features used in captions:
- exclamation
- question
- adjective phrase
- prepositional phrase

3) What is message in caption?
Message in caption is words that someone wants to send to the readers or viewers.

4) Please tell me how to write text accompanying captions!
- Observe the picture or video that you will give the caption.
- Find out the message from the picture before you write the caption.
- Write a sentence or phrase under the picture.
