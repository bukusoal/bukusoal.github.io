11. buatkan percakapan bahasa inggris yang mencakup correlative conjunction (3 orang) ​


Writing Dialog Using Correlative Conjunctions

Jawaban

Pendahuluan

Sebelum membuat dialog mari pahami terlebih dahulu apa pengertiannya. Correlative conjunctions adalah kata hubung seperti and, but, or dan nor yang digunakan berpasangan dengan both, not only, either, dan neither. Beberapa contohnya:

- both me and my sister hate chicken

- not only my mother but also my father love sports

- I want either coffee or tea

- I want neither coffee nor tea

- We will have both roasted chicken and fried chicken

- I didn’t know whether you’d want to take a walk or ride a bicycle

Pembahasan

Kamil: Hey, Martin! What are you up to?

Martin: Hey Kamil. Hmmm I’m not sure what shoes should I bring tomorrow for camping.

Kamil: What’s the problem? I see that you have many shoes.

Martin: Yeah, but here is the problem. Both me and my brother are going to camping tomorrow. Because we share the same size, he would like to take my most comfortable shoes.

Kamil: Ah I see. I think this problem is not only yours but also mine when I was a kid with my older brother.

Martin: Really, how?

Kamil: Yeah, my brother wanted only either my shoes or a new one. He didn’t want to use his shoes. I don’t understand why.

Martin: Apparently both of our brothers are having a little bit weird personality, huh?

Kamil: Haha yeah I agree. By the way let’s go for a food, we will think about how to fix your problem later. Okay.

Martin: Good idea!

21. Buatlah dialog percakapan antara 4 orang di bank dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

Tentu, berikut ini adalah contoh dialog percakapan antara 4 orang di bank dalam bahasa Inggris:

Orang 1 (Teller): Good morning! How can I assist you today?

Orang 2 (Customer 1): Good morning! I'd like to open a savings account, please.

Orang 1 (Teller): Of course, I can help you with that. May I have your identification and initial deposit, please?

Orang 2 (Customer 1): Sure, here's my ID and an initial deposit of $500.

Orang 3 (Customer 2): Excuse me, I have a question about my mortgage payment. Is this the right department to inquire about that?

Orang 1 (Teller): Absolutely, I can help you with mortgage inquiries. Please go ahead and ask your question.

Orang 3 (Customer 2): I'd like to know if it's possible to set up an automatic monthly transfer for my mortgage payment from my checking account.

Orang 1 (Teller): Yes, that can be arranged. Let's discuss the details after I finish assisting Customer 1.

Orang 4 (Customer 3): Hi, I need to cash this check, please.

Orang 1 (Teller): Of course, may I see your ID and the check, please?

Orang 4 (Customer 3): Here's my ID, and the check is for $200.

Orang 1 (Teller): Thank you. I'll process this for you right away. Please have a seat while I take care of these requests.

[The teller proceeds to assist each customer one by one, addressing their needs.]

Ini adalah contoh sederhana percakapan di bank dengan empat orang yang memiliki berbagai keperluan. Di dalam bank, staf biasanya akan membantu setiap pelanggan secara bergantian untuk memastikan semua kebutuhan mereka terpenuhi.
