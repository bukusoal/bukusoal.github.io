4. Buatlah dialog bahasa inggris menggunakan getting one's attention, checking understanding,show appreciation, dan give opinion (untuk 2 orang)​


Jawaban:

Person A: Hey, have you seen my keys?

Person B: (Getting one's attention) Oh, excuse me, I think I saw your keys on the kitchen counter.

Person A: Great! (Checking understanding) Did you put them there?

Person B: No, I didn't. I just happened to notice them there a few minutes ago.

Person A: Thanks a lot for letting me know. (Show appreciation) I really appreciate your help.

Person B: No problem! (Give opinion) It's always good to have someone around who can spot things when they're misplaced.

Questions and Answers about Asking and Giving AttentionJawaban
Pendahuluan

Expressing attention adalah suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk meminta perhatian atau mengajak seseorang untuk fokus perhatiannya pada kita atau orang yang sedang berbicara.

Dalam mengungkapkan attention ada dua macam hal, yakni memberikan (giving) atau meminta (asking).


Contoh asking attention:

- Look!

- Listen!

- Hey!

- Hello?

- Attention, please?

- Excuse me?

- Sorry to bother you


Contoh giving attention:

- Tell me more about it.

- Really?

- What’s next?

- And then what?

- Is that all?

- I see.

Excuse me. (Permisi)

May I have your attention, please? (Boleh saya minta perhatiannya?)

Give me attention, please. (Mohon perhatiannya)

Hi! / Hey / Hello! (Hai!/ halo!)

Look! / look at this! / look at that! ...

Listen! / Listen up! ...

Listen to me for a moment, please.
