19. tolong dijawab soal bahasa Inggris kelas 8​


Jawaban:

1. keep = kept = menjaga

2. pay = Paid = membayar

3. stand = stood = berdiri

4. throw = threw = melemparkan

5. wear = wore = memakai

6. buy = bought = membeli

7. cut = cut = memotong

8. find = found = menemukan

9. forget = forgot = lupa

10. grow = grew = tumbuh

semoga membantu(❁´◡`❁)

Jawaban:

Jawaban:

1. keep - kept - have or retain possession of.

2. pay - Paid - give someone money that is due for work done.

3. stand - stood - have or maintain an upright position.

5. wear - wore - have on one's body or a part of one's body as clothing.

6. buy - bought - obtain in exchange for payment.

7. cut - cut - make an opening.

8. find - found - discover or perceive by chance or unexpectedly.

9. forget - forgot - fail to remember.

10. grow - grew - become larger or greater over a period of time.
