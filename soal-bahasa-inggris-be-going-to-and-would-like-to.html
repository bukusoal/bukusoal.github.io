14. Apa perbedaan penggunaan would like dan to be + going to?


Would like= if you want to do something or say something
To be = when you are sure to do something or be something
Going to= in the upcoming days and the futureWould like to dan be going to merupakan expression intention untuk menyatakan keinginan/niat.
Dan perbedaannya adalah

-Would like
Sama dengan want, hanya saja would like lebih sopan.
Rumus: subject + would like + object/gerund/infinitive
*untuk negatif tambahkan not di depan would.

-Be going to
Hanya bisa digunakan untuk kegiatan yang sudah direncanakan sebelumnya
Rumus: subject + be + going to + verb 1

18. bagaimana pola dalam penggunaan be going to dan would like to​


Jawaban:

BE GOING TO:

Be going to biasa digunakan untuk membicarakan rencana masa depan dimana keputusan tentang rencana tersebut telah dibuat sebelum dikatakan (intention). Selain itu be going to dapat pula untuk memprediksi sesuatu yang kita pikir pasti terjadi atau kita mempunyai bukti atau petunjuk (evidence) pada saat ini.

contoh:

I am going to buy a boat tomorrow.

WOULD LIKE TO:

Would like to biasanya digunakan untuk menawarkan sesuatu. Adapun untuk artinya hampir sama dengan want yakni ingin atau mau. Hanya saja dalam penggunaannya would like to dianggap lebih sopan dan cocok digunakan untuk acara formal atau resmi, berbicara dengan orang yang lebih tua, atas atau lainnya.


19. Writing a short dialogue with "be going to" and "would like to"!


A short dialogue with "be going to" and "would like to"

Joni: "Hi Dino, how are you?

Dino: "I am good, thank you. How about you?

Joni: I am very well, thank you. What are you doing this weekend?

Dino: I am going to go to my friend's birthday party.

Joni: Oh... I see. I would like to invite you to my graduation party if you were free this weekend.

Dino: What time is your party?If it is in the afternoon, I would like to come.

I am free in the afternoon.

Joni: Yes, It is in the lunchtime. Are you able to come?

Dino: Yes, sure. My friend's birthday party is in the evening.

Joni: Ohh...that's great. Thank you.

Dino: My pleasure

Pembahasan

'be going to' biasa digunakan dalam Future Tense, yaitu tenses yang digunakan untuk aktivitas yang akan dilakukan di masa yang akan datang. 'be going to' digunakan untuk aktivitas yang sudah direncanakan sebelumnya dan memprediksikan bahwa sesuatu pasti akan terjadi.

S + be (is/am/are) going to+ verb-1

contoh dalam penggunaan kalimat:

Rina is going to join the English club next month.We are going to clean the classroom next Monday.The students are going to have an exam on Thursday.Dono is going to go to Japan for the next holiday.The baby is going to have a general check up this week.

'Would like to' digunakan untuk menyatakan keinginan atau willingness, dengan kata lain sebagai cara yang lebih sopan dari want. 'Would like to' juga digunakan untuk menawarkan sesuatu. Contoh penggunaan 'would like to' dalam kalimat:

I would like a cup of coffee.Dora would like to come to the general meeting.The children would like to go to the farm for the next field trip.Would you like a cup of tea?Would you like to have dinner now?

26. social function from would like to and be going to


'Would like to' is used to tell someone what somebody wants. For example:

-I would like to have some chocolate ice cream.

-He would like to come with me to the party.

-They would like to have a collaboration with us.

'Be going to' is used to tell someone about where somebody will go in the future. For example:

-You will be going to Nusa Tenggara next month to provide help to the poor citizens.

-She will be going to the beach during the holidays.

-The astronauts will be going to Mars.

37. Perbedaan would like dan to be tambah going to?


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: intention
Pembahasan:

Kedua ungkapan tersebut, baik 'would like' atau 'going to' digunakan utk menyatakan keinginan atau niat (intention).
Perbedaannya adalah
Untuk 'would like' hanya digunakan untuk menyatakan niat sekedarnya atau impian diangan-angan. Misalnya:
I would like to be a doctor when I grow up later. I think it's nice and cool.
Untuk 'going to' lebih digunakan untuk menyatakan niat yang serius dan sudah menuju kearah impian yang akan terwujud. Misalnya:
I'm going to be a doctor in the next few years. I've been studying in medical school now and doing medical internship at the local hospital.
