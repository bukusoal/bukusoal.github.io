8. k tolong jawab soal b inggris kelas 8 hal 37


Perintah soal diminta untuk membuat dua kalimat, yaitu suggestion dan advice. Pola pada suggestion adalah subject + should + v1, sedangkan pola pada advice adalah subject + should + not + v1.

1. We have 2 test tomorrow, English and IPS.

You should not go to the game, you should study for the test

2. I Don't know the meaning of ' row'.

You should look it up in the dictionary yourself, you should not depend on others  all the time

Pembahasan

1. I will go out, but it's very cloudy now.

You should stay at home, you should not go out now or you'll get wet.

2. I think i'm catching a cold.

You should take a rest and drink your medicine regularly, you should not drink something cold.

3. Some students leave litter on their desks.

They should put it in the litter bin, they shouldn't make this room dirty.

4. I sit in the back row. I cannot see your writing on the blackboard.

You should sit in front of the class, you should not do that if you don't wear your glasses.

5. I'm longing for my cousins.

You should ask someone where he is, you should not leave her or him alone.

6. My legs are asleep because i'm sitting on them.

You should sit on the chair, you should not sit on your legs.

7. Our classroom is dirty and messy.

We should clean and tidy up our classroom, we should not let our class like this.

8. I have a toothache.

You should go to the dentist, you should not forget to brush your teeth before sleeping.

9. I can't stop yawning.

You should drink a cup of coffee or move your body, you should not go to bed late every night.

10. I have hiccups.

You should drink a glass of water, you should not let it go so long.

38. Soal bahasa Inggris :Jawab beserta penjelasannya​


Should (Giving Advice)

1. I think they should wait until they finish their study.

2. I think you should stay at home this evening.

3. or should I keep sending his mail to you?

4. You should ask Ivanka, our computer technician, for advice.

5. Everyone should travel.

Should be (Giving Criticism)

1. He should be wearing a coat.

2. It should be open now.

3. Our dog should be running to us [...]

4. They should be coming tonight, [...]

5. They should be in bed. They shouldn't be watching television.

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Should adalah sebuah modal verb yang dapat digunakan untuk memberikan saran. Should mengacu pada aksi yang sebaiknya dilakukan di masa depan.

You have wronged her. You should apologize.You look tired. You should rest.

Should juga digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang diharapkan akan terjadi/menjadi kenyataan.

I should graduate next year.The train should arrive in five minutes.

Should be digunakan untuk mengekspresikan sesuatu yang seharusnya terjadi tapi tidak. Should be mengacu pada masa sekarang (present) atau masa depan (future).

You have a fever. You should be in bed, not working.The President should be giving a speech tonight, but he had to cancel it because of the terrorist attack.

Jadi, should be = is/am/are supposed to
