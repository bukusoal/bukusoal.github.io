Diket

- s = 81GB = 81000mb

- v0 = 45mb/s

- v1 = 35mb/s

- Menjadi glbb diperlambat

- Saat 79% v = v1

t = ?

Jawab

t = s/v

t0 = 81000(79%)/45

t0 = 1422 s

t1 = 81000(100%-79%)/35

t1 = 486 s

t = t0+t1

= 1422+486

= 1908 s

= 31 menit 48 detik
