Posisi

Badan    ;  berdiriTangan  ; letakkan di belakang kepala atau menyilang di dada.kaki        ; lutut agak menekuk

Gerakan

Lakukan jongkok (squat )hingga paha sejajar dengan lantai.
Angkat tubuh atau meloncat (jump) setinggi mungkin dengan mendorong ujung kaki ke lantai.
Saat mendarat, lakukan dengan jari kaki terlebih dahulu lalu bersiap melakukan jongkok lagi.
Lakukan berulang.

28. contoh drama bahasa inggris kelas 8 smpn yang memuat tentang bab1 2 dan 3. yang tau jawab ya :)


Before the subjects began the first morning that Mrs. Linda gave greetings to the students 'good morning kids, how are today, healthier all right?'. The students answered "healthy Mrs. Linda". Suddenly, one of the students who sat the bench behind answered in a slow "I’m less healthy Mrs. Lin". Them Mrs. Linda asked the student "What is up Rina? '. Rina replied "I do not feel well".
Mrs. Linda:Rina otherwise healthy, why not make it a license? Already see a doctor?
Rina:Yes Mrs. Linda, I’ve done. That was actually my intention to not go and make a sick leave, but I do not want to miss today's lesson.
Mrs. Linda:Yes but you're not you're sick. You can always ask your friends about the subject matter these days.
Rina:Yes Mrs. Linda, but I still regret that miss the subject today. Moreover, I actually liked most when taught by Mrs. Linda.
Mrs. Linda:Well then, Rina is now better go home, because it was ill Rina. For today's subject you may ask your friends later. Mrs. Linda would tell Mr. Tono (guard) to deliver Rina home.
Rina:Well, okay Mrs. Linda. Maybe I did have to take a break I'll be a speedy recovery and no longer miss learning activities.
Mrs. Linda:True Rin, Rina now home and resting at home okay!
Rina:Alright Mrs. Linda.
Mrs. Linda then walked out of the classroom Mr. Tono.
Mrs. Linda:Mr. Tono!
Mr. Tono:Yes Mrs. Linda, can I help you?
Mrs. Linda:Mr. Tono, please deliver relevant because Rina home is not feeling well.
Mr. Tono:Okay Mrs. Linda.
Mrs. Linda then call Rina
Mrs. Linda:Rina, Mr. Tono here will take you home.
Rina:Okay Mrs. Linda. Rina was quickly out of the classroom and escorted home by Mr. Tono.
Such examples drama tells the persistence of a student who forces herself to stay in school despite being sick. Hopefully useful and can motivate you to be more active in participating in the educational process
