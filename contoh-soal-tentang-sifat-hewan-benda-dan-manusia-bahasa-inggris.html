Jawaban:

Mary : I’m so happy that we can come to the zoo today (Saya sangat senang kita bisa pergi ke kebun binatang hari ini).

Amanda : Yeah me too, I love seeing all the animals (Ya saya juga, saya suka melihat banyak bintang).

Mary : The tigers look scary, but they are so beautiful (Harimau nya terlihat menyeramkan, tapi mereka sangatlah cantik).

Amanda : I like the monkeys, they are so cute (Saya suka monyet, mereka sangatlah lucu).

Mary : Hi look!! this little bird just landed on my finger, this is cute too (Hai lihat burung kecil ini baru saja berdiri di jari saya, dia lucu juga).

Amanda : Yeah, Which one do you prefer, mammals or birds? (Ya, Mana yang kamu suka mamalia atau burung?)

Mary : I prefer birds , what about you? (Saya lebih suka burung, bagaimana dengan kamu?).

Amanda : Honestly, I really like reptiles and snakes (Sejujurnya, saya sangat suka hewan reptil dan ular).

Mary : Wow that’s cool, but I’m not sure that they have any reptiles here (Wow itu keren, tapi saya tidak yakin kalau ada hewan reptil disini).

Amanda : Yes you’re right, because I don’t see any of it here (Iya kamu benar, karena saya tidak melihat satupun disini).

Mary : Alright then, can you help me please to take me a picture with that parrot? (Baiklah kalau begitu, bisakah kamu membantu saya untuk memotret saya dengan burung beo itu?)

Amanda : Okay I’ll help you. Just wait a moment please (Oke saya akan membantumu. Tunggu sebentar ya).

Mary : Alright, thank you Amanda (Baiklah, terima kasih Amanda).

Amanda : You’re welcome Mary (Sama-sama Mary).

Aldi: Hey Jane, let’s play!

Jane: What game?

Aldi: A riddle. About animals!

Jane: Alright, go ahead.

Aldi: It’s a wild animal, it’s carnivore, it’s living mainly in Africa, Asia (China, India), It’s spotty. What is it?

Jane: Giraffe?

Aldi: What? Giraffe is not a carnivore, you silly!

Jane: Oh wait I forgot. Okay one more time…. Let me think… I think it’s a giant cat?

Aldi: Well yes, it’s felidae.

Jane: Leopard. Correct?

Aldi: Exactly!

Jane: Now, my turn. It’s spiky, the size is small to medium, you can find it in many places like savannas, forests, deserts, scrublands and suburban gardens. Some people have it as a pet too.

Aldi: Hmmm.. If it’s spiky, then it must be hedgehog. Am I right?

Jane: Correct!

Aldi: Yayyy so we’re equal now. Next time we have to continue with harder clue and animal.

Jane: Sure.

Adjective (Kata Sifat)

Kata sifat atau Adjective adalah kata yang memberikan keterangan pada orang atau sesuatu (dapat berupa hewan, benda, dll). Contoh Adjective sudah tertera pada Jawaban diatas.

Sample Text (Contoh Teks yang berisi kata sifat)

  My friend, Joy

I have a friend. Her name is Joy. She has oval face, slanted eyes, straight hair, pointed nose and white complexion.

She is tall, slim, and beautiful. She is clever, diligent and friendly.

⇒ Adjectives :
