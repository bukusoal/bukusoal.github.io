past tense of reguler/irreguler verb
subject + Verb 2 + object

subject + to be (had been) + predicate

19. jelaskan dengan jelas tentang past continuous tense beserta contohnya(semua dalam bahasa inggris)​


Past continuous tense or also commonly known as past progressive tense is a tense that is used to express activities that are happening at a certain time in the past.
The action / activity has been and is ongoing but has not been completed at that time and sometimes also interspersed with an action / activity that occurs briefly.

as example ;
1.my mother 'was' cooking fried rice while I 'was' repairing my laptop.

2.'were' yo u working all night yesterday?

3.rina 'wasn't' doing her home work when Santi called her.

4.we 'weren't' playing outside when the teacher 'came'.

21. buatlah soal pilihan ganda past tense minimal 3..... beserta jawabannya...​

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang telah lalu. Seperti kalimat "Saya telah menulis buku kemarin ( l wrote a book yesterday). Kami telah membuat kue kemarin (we made a cake last time)."

Cara mudah mengerjakan soal Simple past Tense dalam bentuk pilihan ganda adalah dengan cara memahami arti dari kalimat tersebut.

Berikut soal Simple Past Tense pilihan ganda dan jawabannya.

1. Did you .... in my house yesterday ?.(stay)

a. stayed c. staying

b. will stay d. stay

Jawaban : [tex]\colorbox{blue}{Stayed}[/tex].

Kata stay berubah menjadi Stayed karena kalimat menunjukkan kata [tex]\colorbox{blue}{yesterday}[/tex].

2.Did you make this bread tamara? no, I ..... it, I bought it yesterday.(make)

a. didn't make c. will make

b. made d. make

Jawaban : [tex]\colorbox{blue}{didn't~make}[/tex]

karena kalimat menunjukkan keterangan berlawanan (negatif) Apakah kau membuat roti ini tamara? Tidak, saya tidak membuatnya, saya membelinya kemarin.

3.Did you ..... this floor yesterday?.(sweep)

a. sweep c. will sweep

b. swept d. are sweeping

Jawaban : [tex]\colorbox{blue}{swept}[/tex].

kata sweep berubah menjadi swept karena kalimat terdapat kata [tex]\colorbox{blue}{yesterday}[/tex]

Kunci Jawaban :

1. a. stayed

2. a. didn't make

3. b. swept

Simple Past Tense

Apa itu Simple Past Tense?

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat sederhana yang menunjukkan kegiatan/tindakan yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Rumus Simple Past Tense :

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Did + Subject + Infitinitive Verb + Object + ?

Was/Were + Subject + Adjective/Adverb + ?

Contoh :

- Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Example : I met my cousin last week.

She went to the hospital two days ago.

- Subject + Did/Was/Were + Not + Verb 1 + Object

Example : I didn't go to school three days ago.

She wasn't play with her friends last night.

- Did + Subject + Infinitive Verb + Object

Example : Did you go to the market alone?

Did she find something to eat?

- Was/Were + Subject + Adjective + Adverb

Example : Was Alya sad because of him?

Were they bored at home?
