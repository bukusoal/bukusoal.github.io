4. my question ? 1.what is the meaning of report text detail and refrence 2.what is the meaning of agreeing and dis agreeing question from university study english


Jawaban:

1. What is the meaning of report text detail and reference?

Report is a text which presents information about something, as it is. It is as a result of systematic observation and analyses.

Reference is a word, phrase, or idea which comes from something such as a book, poem, or play and which we use when making a point about something.

2. What is the meaning of agreeing and disagreeing?

Agreeing is to express the same opinion

Disagreeing is to express a different opinion
